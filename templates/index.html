Report Abuse. Protect People.

The End Harassment Reporting Wizard automates the process of filing abuse reports against hate sites and harassment campaigns with their hosting providers.

File a Report

Important: Illegal Content

This tool files abuse reports with hosting providers regarding Terms of Service violations such as harassment, hate speech, doxxing, NCII, and copyright infringement.

This tool is not appropriate for reporting illegal content that requires law enforcement action. In particular:

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Report immediately to the NCMEC CyberTipline. Do not upload, download, screenshot, or redistribute CSAM under any circumstances.
  • Imminent threats of violence: Contact your local law enforcement and/or the FBI's Internet Crime Complaint Center (IC3).
  • Other federal cybercrimes: File a report at IC3.gov.

How It Works

  1. Enter URLs

    Provide the URLs of the abusive content you want to report.

  2. Discover Infrastructure

    We automatically identify the hosting providers, IP addresses, and abuse contacts responsible for serving the content.

  3. Provide Evidence

    Link to screenshots or other evidence documenting the abuse, and classify the violation type.

  4. Review & Submit

    Review the generated abuse report and submit it for admin approval. Once approved, it is sent to the appropriate abuse contacts.
